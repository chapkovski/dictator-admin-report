Instructions

The closer will be your guess about how much others will keep for themselves, the more you will earn.

If your guess will be exactly the same as an average amount the other participants kept for themselves, you will earn 20 points.

Every point of different will decrease your payoff by 1 point.

If the difference betweeen real average amount kept by others and your guess is more than 20 tokens you will receive nothing from this stage.
